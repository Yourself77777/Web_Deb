---Bear Lake---
Life's a beach, but this lake's better

Bear Lake, straddling the Utah and Idaho border, is an essential destination famous for its remarkable, vibrant turquoise-blue water often nicknamed the "Caribbean of the Rockies." This unique coloration stems from suspended limestone deposits, creating a stunning visual backdrop for abundant year-round recreational opportunities like boating, swimming, and snowmobiling. The region is also renowned for its delicious local raspberries, which are celebrated during the annual Raspberry Days festival. Overall, Bear Lake offers visitors a unique blend of natural beauty, outdoor adventure, and local flavor that makes it a memorable trip.
